Interests

Quadrature Dev is interested in the intersection of digital signal processing and data science, especially applied to problems with radio and wireless communications. We are interested in open source and have experience with PyTorch, GNU Radio, and accelerated instruction sets like SSE, AVX, NEON, and CUDA for GPUs.

Skills

Quadrature Dev has specialty in software development in C, C++, python, rust, and julia. We have targetted specialized instruction sets including CUDA for NVIDIA GPUs; SSE, AVX, AVX2, AVX512 for x86_64, NEON for ARM. We're familiar working around low-probability of intercept and detection waveforms and signal detectors including feature analysis and machine-learning methods. We have also contributed to full software modems for various waveforms and are comfortable with profiling tools for software optimization.
